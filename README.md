## Project Overview

This project analyzes e-commerce sales data using Microsoft Excel and Power Query. The objective was to clean raw transaction data, transform it into an analysis-ready format, and build an interactive dashboard to track key business metrics such as revenue, orders, product performance, payment methods, and order status.

The project demonstrates end-to-end data analysis skills, including data cleaning, transformation, KPI creation, dashboard development, and business insight generation.

## Feature Engineering

Additional business metrics were created:

### Total Revenue

Calculated as:

Revenue = Quantity × Price

### Month

Extracted from Order Date for trend analysis.

### Year

Created for time-based reporting.

### Revenue Bucket

Grouped transactions into revenue ranges.

### Status Group

Created standardized order status categories.

## Key Insights

### Revenue by Category

* Books generated the highest revenue among all product categories.
* Electronics contributed the lowest revenue.

### Product Performance

* Shoes emerged as the highest revenue-generating product.
* Comics and Lamp were also among the top-performing products.

### Payment Analysis

* Cash on Delivery was the most preferred payment method.
* Digital payment methods such as PayPal and Bank Transfer followed closely behind.

### Order Status Analysis

* A significant proportion of orders remained In Progress.
* Unsuccessful orders represented a sizeable share of total orders, indicating potential opportunities to improve order fulfillment processes.

### Revenue Trend

* Monthly revenue showed fluctuations throughout the year.
* Revenue peaks were observed around February, June, and October.
